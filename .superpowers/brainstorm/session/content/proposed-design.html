Proposed Setup Design

A 4-step wizard to get everything running perfectly.

1. Welcome
Force Ethernet Tethering needs a few permissions to monitor your connection and automate the settings toggle.
2. Notifications
Required to keep the service running reliably in the background.
3. Accessibility
The core of the app. We'll show you exactly which menu item to click.
4. Battery (Optional)
Prevents Android from killing the app when the screen is off.
